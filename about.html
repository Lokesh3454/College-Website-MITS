About MITS College

Madanapalle Institute of Technology & Science - A Premier Engineering Institution

Our Mission

MITS College is dedicated to providing quality technical education and fostering innovation in engineering and technology. We prepare students to be competent engineers, entrepreneurs, and leaders who contribute to society's technological advancement.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in technical education, research, and innovation, producing globally competent engineers and technologists who contribute to sustainable development and societal progress.

Quality Education

AICTE approved programs with industry-relevant curriculum and experienced faculty.

Research & Innovation

State-of-the-art laboratories and research centers promoting innovation and entrepreneurship.

Industry Connect

Strong industry partnerships ensuring excellent placement opportunities and practical exposure.
